Emergency and Natural Disaster Perceptions and Preparedness

The purpose of this data visualization project is to provide students with another means to view their collected survey data, visualize the results, and be able to formulate research questions for their own projects. This data can also be used to view the feelings of preparedness, perceptions of disasters and management, and demographic information of respondents within a users location of interest.
